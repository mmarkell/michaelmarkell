-r (Dashr) Backend

Best Web Service, Hack@Brown 2015
View source code.



Dashr is a web application that helps you "find your herd." (cute, right?) Imagine Tinder, but for everything else. Want to find people to go kayaking with? What about underwater basket weaving? Dashr could be for you!

Created with Kenji Endo and Miranda Chao at Hack@Brown 2015, February 7 - 8, 2015. I did back end development and some UI design for this project. I coded in HTML5, CSS, Python, SQL.

Shoutout to Mackenzie Clark, Molly Long, and many other Hack@Brown alum mentors for their incredible support and mentorship in our first hackathon.
