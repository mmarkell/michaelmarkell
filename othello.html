Othello.java

Final Project - CS015 (Object Oriented Programming)
Request Source Code.



Othello is a boardgame that is relatively trivial to implement, excluding the AI. The game itself is similar to checkers.

I wrote a recursive minimax function that optimizes your move based on negating an oponent's optimal score. In short, the algorithm picks the move that is worst for your opponent (best for you.)

I wrote minimax to be extensible to multiple levels of intelligence. I hardcoded three levels of intelligence, but the algorithm is easily extensible to even smarter players
