Declaration of Standard Concentration Program

There are instructions throughout this page that should guide you as you work through the declaration process. Note that some concentrations have custom fields and requirements, so if you switch from one concentration to another, some details in this screen may change.

Remember that the editability of a declaration will change during the course of the declarations process. Students can only edit a declaration when its status is either "Not Submitted" or "Revisions Requested." Advisors can only approve a declaration when its state is "Pending," after a student submits it for review.
